Love story



- Tereza -

„From my point of view, my love for David wasn't at first sight. Talking on the first date about math, computers and running is not a turn on for me. It took a while to find out, how heartwarming, caring and loving person he is. He would do everything for others and never leave you helpless. We have been together since 2016 and even though we are totally different, I feel like at home wherever I am with him. You want to be with that kind of person for the rest of your life. I am glad we found each other so soon in our life and that we can spend the rest of it together.“



- David -


„My love for Tereza has always been a love on first sight for me. I was so excited when we met that I had to tell her about everything that interested. When I was talking about my interests and my life, she always carefully listened and was interested in me. At the same time, however, whenever Tereza started talking, every single thought of her was precise for me. I was just the more talkative one! But it has already been pointed out to me. And it is important to remember that. It is important to let others talk too! The most important think for me is that she is making me a better man and all my success I have had I am grateful to her. She has always been supportive when I kept returning from job interviews empty and our living together did not look promising. Tereza has always been optimistic and stayed strong for both of us. Our future together would not exist without such support of her, that I am sure of. When someone feels that safe with someone and feels that the loved one is making him or her a better person, it is already decided. Without Tereza big part of me would be missing, the better one. And I have never felt this way with anyone before. And that is why I decided to propose to her. Successfully!“

How you get there

The best way to get there is by car. The best bus link is probably through Znojmo. The closest bus stop is Kravsko, near blacksmith. If you don't have the option to get there by car, please, let us know, we might be able to set up some carpooling for you. You can find the address of the place little higher where the wedding events is, when you click on the button SHOW ON MAP.

Accommodation place

The barn Suška accommodates in a hotel which belongs to Kocanda, Kravsko. It is about 2 km away from the barn. However, please, come into the area of Kocanda, the ride between those two places will be dealt with so everyone can drink and enjoy the wedding, even eternal drivers :) On the wedding day you can accommodate after 12 p.m., second day the checkout is at 10 a.m.. Please, let us know about the accommodation, we will take care of everything else. Accommodation price is around 39 Euroes / 45 US Dollars per person. (we will let you know in time).

Dresscode

Our wedding theme will be a rustic style combined with semiformal dresscode. For men that means full dress with either a suit or a vest with nice dress shoes and possibly either a tie or a bowtie. Jeans, sneakers and similar clothes and shoes don't belong in this dresscode. For ladies that means a dress, or a skirt and blouse. If you would like to, rustic style and the place itself is all about autumn colors and flowers. This hint might suit you if you would like your pictures to stand out and match with the whole wedding atmosphere :). Also, we would like to kindly ask you if you could leave white dress at home and let it be only the bride's color.
